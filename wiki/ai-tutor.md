# AI Tutor — Product Concept

**Summary**: An AI-powered mathematics study coach/tutor built on the Ugandan National Curriculum (NCDC). The UG curriculum specificity is the defensible moat — generic AI tutors exist, curriculum-specific ones do not.

**Sources**: [[raw/AI Engineer Chapter 1 (Chip Huyen, 2025).md|AI Engineering Chapter 1]]

---

## Product Overview

**Type**: Consumer/Enterprise Education AI
**Curriculum**: Uganda National Curriculum (NCDC)
**Primary Use**: Mathematics tutoring aligned to the UG national curriculum

## Why This Is Defensible

- Generic AI tutors (Khanmigo, ChatGPT-based tutors) exist — they cannot replace a curriculum-specific tutor
- The **NCDC curriculum** as a structured knowledge base gives the product unique grounding
- UG-specific context (local language needs, term structure, exam pacing) is not in global model training data

## AI Engineering Chapter 1 Framing

- Listed under **Education** in Table 1-3: Tutoring (consumer), Employee onboarding/upskilling (enterprise)
- Education is explicitly called out as a high-value, early-stage category
- Chapter 1 frames the question: should you build it? — a curriculum-specific tutor has a clear *yes* answer because of the moat
